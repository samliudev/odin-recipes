Ichiraku Ramen

Ramen is the Best Pleasure

Learn to make the Naruto's go-to ramen from his favorite restaurant!

Ingredients

  • 4 cups of Cooked Ramen
  • 5 cups of your favorite Meat Broth
  • Pork Tenderloin Cutlet
  • 2 Eggs
  • 1 Baby Bok Choy
  • 1 cup of Scallions
  • .5 cups of Soy Sauce

Steps

  1. Preheat your stove to 450 degrees.
  2. Cook the pork cutlet for 10-15 minutes.
  3. Boil your eggs for 10 minutes in a separate pot of water. Place in cold water when done.
  4. Pour your broth into a pot and let it simmer. Add in soy sauce.
  5. When the broth is beginning to boil, add in the ramen noodles.
  6. To prepare the toppings, peel the eggs and slice in half. Cut the tenderloin, boy choy, and scallions into slices
  7. When the noodles are finished cooking, pour the broth and noodles into a bowl.
  8. Place your toppings over the noodles and enjoy!
