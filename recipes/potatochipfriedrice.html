Potato Chip Fried Rice

Probably Don't Make this for Your Mother

If you have leftover chips and extra rice, here's something to try!

Ingredients

  • 200 grams of Rice
  • 16 grams of Potato Chips
  • 1 Egg
  • 1/4 Onion
  • 1/4 pack of Sprouts
  • 1 teaspoon Soy Sauce
  • 1/2 teaspoon Chicken Stock Powder
  • 1 teaspoon Grated Ginger
  • 1 tablespoon Sesame Oil/li>

Steps

  1. Remove the stems from the sprouts. Finely chop the onions and the sprouts.
  2. Pour the sesame oil and ginger into a heated pan on low heat.
  3. Add in the onions and cook on medium until they are softened.
    4. ill softened
  4. Add in the rice, half of the sprouts, soy sauce and chicken stock.
  5. After frying, mix in crushed potato chips and remove from heat.
  6. Cover a bowl in saram wrap and pour in the rice. Flip the bowl onto a plate to get a nice half-sphere shape.
  7. Separate egg yolk from egg whites. Create a dimple in the rice and top with egg yolk. Spread the remaining sprouts over the top of the dish and serve.
